About Harry Potter

Harry Potter is the central character in J.K. Rowling's famous series of books. A young wizard, Harry learns about his magical heritage on his 11th birthday.

Throughout the series, Harry faces numerous challenges, including the dark wizard Voldemort, who seeks to dominate the wizarding and Muggle worlds.

Harry's bravery, loyalty, and friendships are central themes of the story, inspiring millions of readers around the globe.
